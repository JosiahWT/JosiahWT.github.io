The Robot Uprising

By Josiah WT

What is the Robot Uprising?

Since the very beginning of the usage of the word robot as a machine there has always been the fear of them over throwing their makers. The very first example of the word robot as a machine slave was in a play were they overthrough there makers. The first major film to popularize the idea of machines that are humanoid was Star Wars though. And soon after the movie Terminator. After these two films the idea of metal man became a mainstream thing. And after star wars most depictions of robots are as villians. And this is one of the very strong reasons why man fears machines.

Will The Robot Uprising ever happen or has it already happened?

Unlikely the ability to feel emotion and have a higher intelligence is a very hard thing to replicate. And even if we had the secret code to create machines that can think, why would we do it. To elaborate, why would we give machines the ability to feel emotions? To be frank there is no reason and even if there were a reason no one in their right mind would do it. So now that we no longer have to fear machines taking over, we can talk about the fear of machines already ruling the world. The movie the matrix popularized the idea of us living in a fake world. And this is an insane idea to us, but it's also impossible to prove wrong. So I guess it is possible for robots to already rule the world, but it is also impossible for robots to take over the world.
